ABOUT

I think i have more ideas and not enough patience to execute them which is great for coming up with original concepts but makes me lesser of a designer. I have tried to highlight my creative capabilities with this website, but will allow you to judge for yourself.

I did not apply mobile first principles in the design so please be aware it looks best on a wide screen monitor for the sake of attempting a wow factor. Bread winner to my family from the age of 15, nothing ever came easy. This has allowed me to truly appreciate the fruits of a hard won reward. How ever things do catch up which eventually led to a burnout in 2014. I am now in recovery, however not yet fully healed especially due to current affairs.

Diligent in my work and striving for excellence. The difference between respect and pride to me is a solid foundation built on rewards gained by earning through hard work and determination. Sure, its an unfair world in which our efforts can be tarnished by people who don't mind what consequences their actions have on others and who use as if the wheel never turns. But whats keeping my flame alive is understanding the difference between teaching a parrot to mimic human sounds and teaching a baby to talk. One is entertainment for bird brains, one is nurturing for thriving.

Somehow I still have faith in the best of humanity even though I have seen the worst. Perhaps it makes me seem dumb, but destruction and disruption leads only to chaos without the possibility of growth. When i see grandiose art, architecture, and wonders it does remind me that we have so much within us.

Thats enough of the sentiment. Here is what more about me as a worker.

- I have 5 years experience in retail ecom.
- Responsive frameworks and digital content.
- Content management through services such as Trello and Jira
- Wire-frame and user mapping
- Social media and email marketing
- Im adept in html and css but am not a strong coder.
- I mainly create content in Adobe products but have knowledge of free software such as Blue Griffin and Gravit designer.
- I create static content as well as interactive content (honestly speaking very basic video editing and animation,
but a quick learner)

Other skills I have are Style guide design
- Logo design
- Illustration
- Some drawing and painting.

When a company talks about team work I already get the heebeejeebees. I'm quite introverted and when I attempt to form part of the crowd I tend to stand out like a sore thumb. So the environment in which I deliver the best work is within a respectful environment where workers are hired for what they can bring to the table n0t for who has the most game.
